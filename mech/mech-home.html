CAD stands for Computer Aided Drafting and Design, and is useful in multiple aspects of engineering. Many times, when students look at a robot, they are interested in driving it, or becoming hands-on, and using tools to construct and compose the robot. But, every aspect of the build process is important, including the design. On this page, you will find resources on learning how to get started with CAD and ways to approach the design process
